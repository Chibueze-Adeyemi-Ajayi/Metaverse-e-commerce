• Web3 Connection: Meta is a Web3 based Distributed App (DApp) i.e It's built over the blockchain. To get using this app, it's expected you have an injected Web3 on your device - which you can follow these steps to get started:

    • Desktop/Laptop:
      • Install MetaMask or any trusted equivalent Web3 extension on your browser
      • While installation is successful, run the extension - import or create your wallet
      • Open Meta on that browser, then click "Get Started"
      • A little window will appear shortly, kindly accept the process
      • Once connected, you're free to enjoy the decentralized world


    • Mobile Devices:
      • Visit your app/play store, install Metamask or equivalent Web3 injector you trust
      • Create your account and wallet, do keep your mnemonics safe
      • Locate the application menu (side-bar in MetaMask), click on browser/browse
      • Paste https://meta.jilo.com.ng in the search box, then click enter
      • A connection process will begin, kindly permit this process
      • Once done, Hurray !!! ... time to enjoy the decentralized world
